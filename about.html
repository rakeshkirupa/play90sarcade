About This Website

Welcome to Retro 90s Games.

This website is created for people who grew up in the 90s and want to relive classic childhood games directly in their browser.

Our goal is to bring back the nostalgia of simple retro games such as Snake, Tetris, Brick Breaker, Hangman and other classics that defined early gaming experiences.

All games on this website are lightweight browser-based games designed purely for fun and nostalgia.

This project is maintained as a personal retro gaming project to celebrate classic games from the early days of mobile phones and arcade machines.

If you have suggestions or feedback about the games or website, feel free to reach out through the contact page.
